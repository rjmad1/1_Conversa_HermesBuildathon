We built **Conversa** for Buildathon: a governance-first meeting intelligence prototype.

Most meeting tools stop at summaries.
Conversa goes one step further:
✅ Extract decisions, risks, and actions
✅ Keep a human approval gate
✅ Preserve auditable workflow events
✅ Enforce tenant/workspace boundaries in verified scenarios

What’s real today:
- Hono + Vite implementation
- transcript-to-action flow
- approval/rejection + audit
- security remediation evidence (tests + adversarial + smoke)

What’s not claimed yet:
- production authentication
- durable enterprise persistence
- fully verified live connector suite
