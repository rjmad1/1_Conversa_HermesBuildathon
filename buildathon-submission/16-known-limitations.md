# Known Limitations

- No production authentication.
- Development identity can be spoofed (header-based scope in dev mode).
- In-memory persistence only; no durable tenant data.
- No direct meeting-platform connector in stable path.
- External integrations are incomplete/planned.
- Live AI dependencies can fail; stable demo uses synthetic transcript.
- No production-grade observability stack.
- No verified rate-limiting/abuse controls as production feature.
- No formal compliance certification.
- No enterprise scale/load testing evidence.
- No disaster recovery implementation.
- Vercel public verification is external to this Hermes lane.
