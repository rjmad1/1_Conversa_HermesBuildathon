## 👁️ Product Vision

Conversa transforms audio recordings and unstructured conversation streams into structured, deterministic, and audience-tailored executive outputs, action registers, risk matrices, and dynamic living workspace knowledge graphs. 

Instead of passive transcription summaries, Conversa acts as an active **Cognitive Operating System** that validates claims, resolves agent contradictions, signs knowledge manifests with 3-hash cryptographic lineage, and directly manages project workspace objects.

---

## 👥 Target Personas & User Journeys

### 1. Executive Leader / Director
* **Goal**: Understand decisions, major strategic risks, and top action items without reading lengthy meeting transcripts.
* **Journey**: Accesses Executive Summaries and Stakeholder Briefs generated by Phase 3 Semantic Publication Bus; reviews consensus confidence scores and verified cryptographic manifests.

### 2. Engineering Manager / Tech Lead
* **Goal**: Convert meeting discussions into actionable tasks, track architectural dependencies, and dispatch work items directly into issue trackers.
* **Journey**: Uses spatial workspace command surface to view Action Registers, trigger native hand-offs to GitHub/Jira/Linear/Azure DevOps/Slack, and inspect Living Graph dependency topologies.

### 3. Compliance & Security Officer
* **Goal**: Ensure meeting recordings and extracted knowledge adhere to strict data privacy guardrails and audit controls.
* **Journey**: Verifies 5-tier privacy classifications (`Public`, `Internal`, `Confidential`, `Restricted`, `Regulated`), inspects 3-hash audit logs (`semanticHash`, `contentHash`, `provenanceHash`), and monitors data residency boundaries (`US`, `EU`, `India`, `Global`, `CustomerManaged`, `AirGapped`).

### 4. Autonomous AI Agent / Assistant
* **Goal**: Query workspace knowledge, participate in A2A (Agent-to-Agent) negotiations, and execute context-aware RAG searches.
* **Journey**: Interacts via MCP (Model Context Protocol) endpoints (`/platform/mcp`), registers AegisOS interaction traces, and performs vector RAG queries over Convex indexed stores.
